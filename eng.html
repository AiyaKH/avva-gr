About Us


Welcome to our company «ТОО AVVA Technologies»

The company has been operating in the market since 2017 and is part of the international holding
«Confiaza Logistics» — Spain.


Our company — It is a dynamic player in the technology market, rapidly evolving to provide clients with cutting-edge business solutions.

We strive to leverage advanced technologies and automation, including artificial intelligence and data analytics, to optimize processes and ensure high efficiency and reliability for our clients.

Our team consists of experienced professionals ready to develop customized solutions tailored to the specific needs of each client.

  • We conduct audits with the aim of improving, enhancing, and optimizing processes and costs in areas such as HR, IT, HSE (Health, Safety, and Environment), and logistics (planning, warehousing, delivery, and more).

  • We develop digital and logistics strategies, as well as regulations and instructions, based on the processes and requirements of the Client.

  • Using the obtained data, we offer the latest and most effective technologies, equipment, and resources to achieve long-term positive results.
